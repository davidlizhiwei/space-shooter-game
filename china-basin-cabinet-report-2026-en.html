1. Industry Overview

1.1 Definition & Classification

Basin Integrated Cabinet, also known as bathroom cabinet or washbasin unit, is an integrated bathroom furniture product combining washbasin, cabinet, mirror and other functions. Based on materials and craftsmanship, it can be classified into:

  • Ceramic Integrated Basin (60% market share): High-temperature fired, smooth surface, easy to clean, never stains
  • Sintered Stone Basin (25% market share): Mohs hardness 6-7, wear-resistant and heat-resistant, strong design appeal
  • Artificial Stone Basin (10% market share): High plasticity, rich colors, cost-effective
  • Others (5%): Glass, stainless steel, natural stone, etc.

1.2 Industry Development History

China's basin integrated cabinet industry has experienced three stages: OEM & Export (2000-2010), Brand Rise (2010-2020), and Smart Upgrade (2020-present). In 2024, China's bathroom cabinet production accounted for 65% of global output, making it the world's largest producer and exporter.

üí° Key Insight: The industry is transforming from "functional carrier" to "space aesthetics + smart hub". Smart bathroom cabinet penetration rate increased rapidly from 8% in 2022 to 23% in 2025.

3. Industry Chain Analysis

3.1 Upstream - Raw Materials

Ceramic: Mainly produced in Foshan, Chaozhou (Guangdong), Tangshan (Hebei). China's ceramic output accounts for over 60% of global production.
Sintered Stone: Imported (Italy, Spain) + Domestic (Guangdong, Shandong). Domestic share reached 55% in 2025.
Boards: Solid wood, multi-layer boards, PVC, etc. Environmental standards upgraded to E0 and ENF grades.
Hardware: Hinges, slides, faucets, etc. Main production bases in Kaiping (Guangdong) and Wenzhou (Zhejiang).

3.2 Midstream - Manufacturing

China has over 3,000 bathroom cabinet manufacturers, mainly concentrated in Guangdong (Foshan, Zhongshan), Zhejiang (Hangzhou, Taizhou), Fujian (Quanzhou), and Sichuan (Chengdu). Foshan, Guangdong is known as the "Capital of Bathroom Cabinets in China", accounting for 45% of national production.

5. Development Trends

5.1 Technology Trends

  • Smart Features: Smart mirror cabinets (anti-fog, lighting, Bluetooth), sensor faucets, constant temperature water output
  • Antibacterial: Antibacterial ceramic, nano-coating, silver-ion antibacterial
  • Customization: Whole-house customization extension, personalized size, color, and functions
  • Eco-friendly: ENF-grade boards, water-based paint, recyclable materials

5.2 Product Trends

  • Small Apartment Special: Mini models under 60cm, space-saving
  • Sintered Stone Popular: Sintered stone countertop + ceramic basin combination, combining aesthetics and practicality
  • Cream/Minimalist Style: Low-saturation colors like cream white and beige gray
  • Wall-mounted: Hidden drainage, easy to clean, aesthetically pleasing

5.3 Channel Trends

  • Live E-commerce: Douyin and Kuaishou becoming new growth points
  • Turnkey Channels: Deep cooperation with decoration companies
  • Lower-tier Markets: County-level markets becoming new blue ocean
  • Overseas Expansion: Cross-border e-commerce + overseas warehouse model
