1. Industry Overview

Definition & Classification

Basin Integrated Cabinet (Bathroom Vanity) integrates basin, cabinet, and mirror functions. Classification by material:

  • Ceramic (60%): High-temperature firing, smooth, easy to clean
  • Sintered Stone (25%): Mohs 6-7 hardness, wear-resistant, design appeal
  • Artificial Stone (10%): Strong plasticity, rich colors
  • Others (5%): Glass, stainless steel, natural stone
